The Characters
This is the legendary Squad Seven
This is Naruto. He is the main character. He is known as the No. 1 Hyperactive, Knucklehead Ninja.

He dreams of becoming Hokage, to win the respect of all the village. He is an orphan, and has the spirit of a demon trapped inside of him (there is a seal don't worry). No one respects him, and they all treat him like some kind of nobody. He was alone most of his life.

This is Sasuke. Let's say he is Naruto's rival and best friend. He is from the Uchiha Clan, from which he is the only survivor.

He doesn't talk much, and is focused on killing a certain person, thus achieving vengance. Many girls like him.

This is Sakura, arguably the most useless character. She practically does nothing, apart from liking Sasuke and crying.

And this is Kakashi, arguably one of the coolest characters.

He has a very laid back attitude and was considered a genius from a very young age.

He posseses the Sharingan, a special ability, and has a scar on his eye, which makes him even more badass.
Some other cool ass characters
This guy is named Neji. He has the cool ability called the Byakugan. He can enable super-vision.

Neji is great guy, he is intelligent, and good in fights. He also has gorgeous long hair.

This is Rock Lee. He has a talent in Taijustsu (that is, hand to hand combat). He wants to prove himself to Guy Sensei.

He is extremely loyal and is in a way, a great comedic relief.

This is Shikamaru. He has an IQ of over 200, but... he is kind of a lazy guy. Nevertheless, he is a great tactician and leader.

He always complains about evertything, and does nothing to stop it.

But he thinks greatly of others. (Plus we stan his earings)

This is Gaara. He is a bit peculiar: he has a sand demon trapped insde of him.

He always carries a gourd of sand around. It acts as a protective barrier, and as a weapon. It responds without even Gaara's instructions. He is so cool with his deep low voice, and eyelinered eyes (actually, it's because he does not sleep but..yeah).

Finishing with this guy, Iruka Sensei. He is one of the teachers at the Academy.

He was the first one to aknowledge Naruto and becomes like a father-figure to him. He showed Naruto what it was like to have people that cared about you. He is the firt one who ever believed in Naruto.
