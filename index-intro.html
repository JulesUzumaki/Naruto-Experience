The Five Great Shinobi Nations are the biggest countries of the Naruto world. They have been divided equally to keep the balance of the Shinobi world. Nevertheless, the land of Fire is considered the most influencial of all. In each of the nations there is an extremely powerful shinobi village. In the land of Fire, it is the Village Hidden In the Leaves, where most of the Naruto Series take place.

Shinobi

The terms "ninja" and "shinobi" are used interchangeably. A ninja will perform missions for a fee. The mission can invlude manual labor, acting as escorts, gathering secret information, retrieving stolen items, carrying out assassinations, espionnage etc... Most of the shinobi come from a hidden village. Everybody cn become a shinobi. During times of war, a shinobi is expected to defend its village, even if it costs its life.
Note: the plural form of "ninja" and "shinobi" does not take a final -s.

Ninjutsu

Ninjustu is one on the three main categories of jutsu (technique), along with Genjutsu and Taijustsu. Most ninjutsu require chakra and hand seals, but this is not always the case since the mere usage of weaponry qualifies as ninjutsu. This is the most used type of jutsu. Using chakra, you can create fire, clones, and even a ball of lightning.

Taijutsu

Taijutsu is, simply put, hand-to-hand combat. You don't really need chakra for this, even though it can help sometimes. You need a lot of stamina and strength to get better at Taijustsu, as well as knowledge in martial arts, such as Kung Fu. In short, all the punches and kicks fall in this category of jutsu.

Genjutsu

This type of jutsu uses chakra. the effects of genjutsu are not real, being only sensory illusions experienced by those who fall victim to it. It creates an another dimmension. It isn't a physical jutsu. Nevertheless, it can traumatize and play with the mind of your opponent. As a result, those placed under genjutsu for extended amounts of time can suffer from exhaustion and weariness from mental stress.

Chakra

The Chakra Pathway System is a term used to describe the point within the body that channel chakra through it. Simply put they serve the same function for chakra as blood vessels do for blood. Chakra is essential to even the most basic jutsu. Through various methods, the most common of which is hand seals, chakra can be controlled and manipulated to create an effect that would not be possible otherwise, such as walking on water, exhaling fire, or creating illusions.

Ranks
Student

The first step to becoming a shinobi is the Academy. It's like a school, you have a Sensei (teacher) and classmates. You have to learn the basics of being a shinobi and master the basics of some jutsu. You also have to learn chakra control. Once ready, you can take the graduation exam. If you pass, you become a Genin, the lowest ranked ninja. If you fail, you have to stay at the Academy.

Genin

Provided you have passed the Graduation exam, you can become a Genin ! Woww not so fast. You have to pass one last exam... You will be put in a squad with two other students. A Jonin will be assifn to your squad. He will test you capacities to determine whether the three students are worthy of being officially promoted into genin, sent back to the Academy for further training. The Jonin can evaluate as he wishes. If you manage to pass.. Great ! You can now wear proudly the handband of your village.

Chunin

After some time, your Sensei can recommend you to the Chunin exams. They are pretty hard, and contains writtens exams, as well as tournaments. Once you become Chunin, you can wear the special green vest. It's pretty hard though.

Jonin

This rank is the rank of the elite ninja, the highest class. They go on the most difficult missions and sometimes carries them out alone. They can be assigned to a squad of three Genin, and become their Sensei. They are generally highly experienced and highly skilled shinobi who serve as military captains.

The Hokage

Well this isn't really a rank, it's more a title given to the leader of the village. The Hokage is generally regarded as the strongest shinobi in the village. They have their face carved in the Hokage Rock. They are very respected, and becoming one is Naruto's dream.

Clans

Well as you can guess, a clan is basically a family of many shinobi. Usually a renowned one. Inside of a clan usually flows a special ability (Kekkei Genkai) or a secret family Jutsu. This makes them feared by other villages and powerful.

Nara Clan

For example the Nara Clan are known for their intelligence and abilities in medecine. They are also known for they ability to manipulate shadows with the Shadow Possession Jutsu.

Aburame Clan

Another example is the Aburame Clan. They are isect specialists. At birth, they are offered as a nest for insects. These insects live under their skin. Their relationship is mutual, the insects have shelter, and in return the user can bid them orders. These insects feed on enemy chakra, making them deadly.

Kekkei Genkai

A Kekkei Genkai is an anomaly of the DNA that allows the wielder to use unique techniques. That means the user amy be able to do things that are normally impossible, such as growing bones out of his body or X-ray vision. Kekkei Genkai make the user extremely powerful. Most kekkei genkai are passed down between generations of a clan. Usually, a clan will fost a special Kekkei Genkai that many members have. This is what distinguishes them.

Byakugan

This is the Kekkei Genkai of the Huyga Clan. Individuals with a Byakugan have a near-360º field of vision around themselves. The Byakugan's vision can penetrate almost any object, allowing users to see through walls, peer underground, or even examine the contents of a person's body by detecting the chakra in it. It can even focus on something very very far and follow it.

Sharingan

This is the Kekkei Genkai of the Uchiha Clan. The Uchiha Clan was a very influential clan. It's Sasuke's clan. The user is able to copy almost any jutsu they see, memorising ninjutsu, genjutsu, and taijutsu with near-perfect accuracy. They have a perfect vision, which helps them predict the movement of an opponent. They can anticipate an opponent's next move based on the slightest muscle tension in their body and act accordingly to dodge or intercept. Having quick reflexes and predicting the mouvements can lead to the opponent thinking the user can "see the future".
